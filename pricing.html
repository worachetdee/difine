Frequently Asked Questions

help What's included in the course fee?

Your membership covers the dinner course at your assigned restaurant. Drinks, additional dishes, and guest courses are charged separately at the venue.

help Can I choose which restaurant I visit?

Silver members receive AI-curated assignments. Gold and Diamond members can choose from multiple vetted options each month.

help What if I need to cancel?

Cancel within your tier's window (1 week/3 days/1 day before) at no charge. Late cancellations forfeit that month's dining credit.

help Can I bring guests?

Yes! Invite guests at an additional per-person course fee. Silver allows 1 guest, Gold allows 2, Diamond allows 3.

help Is this a contract?

Memberships are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime before your next billing cycle.

help How do you select restaurants?

We partner with Michelin-listed and chef-driven establishments. Our algorithm matches you based on cuisine preferences and availability.
